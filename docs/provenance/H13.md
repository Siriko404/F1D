## E. Design Decisions

1. **Two-tailed test:** No strong prior on direction — uncertainty could increase CapEx (option-value investment) or decrease it (precautionary delay). User chose two-tailed.
2. **CapexAt excluded from ALL controls:** Follows H4 pattern (Lev excluded even for Lev_lead DV). CapexAt is the DV family; including it as a control when DV=CapexAt_lead would create near-tautological regression.
3. **Fiscal year time index:** Old H13.1 used calendar year `year`; fixed to `fyearq_int` to match DV construction (Q4 capxy is a fiscal year variable).
4. **Moulton problem acknowledged:** CapexAt is constant within firm-fiscal-year (one annual value for all calls in that fiscal year). Firm-clustered SEs partially mitigate. Table notes disclose.
5. **Clarity residuals:** Coverage is 43.8% (CEO) and 60.2% (Manager) — lower than uncertainty measures (~70-97%). Complete-case filtering reduces sample from ~88K to ~37K.
