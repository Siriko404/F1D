## N. Final Readiness Statement

**Can this suite be reproduced end-to-end from code alone?**
Mostly yes, with the caveat that H0.3 clarity residuals must be pre-computed (no orchestrator enforces ordering), and `get_latest_output_dir()` depends on stale directories not interfering. Finance/Utility regression summary txt files are not produced despite models running.

**Can a committee/referee understand exactly how the variables are built?**
Yes -- variable construction is well-documented with code references. The firm_maturity docstring error is the only documentation discrepancy.

**Are the implemented results trustworthy for descriptive inference?**
Only with major caveats. The descriptive association between Lev_lag and Presentation uncertainty is present in the Main sample (2/8 significant), but the full temporal pattern (Lev_lead >> Lev_lag in subsamples) undermines even the descriptive claim by suggesting the association runs in the wrong direction.

**Are they trustworthy for causal inference?**
No. The Lev_lead falsification failure is devastating: future leverage predicts current speech better than past leverage, which is inconsistent with any backward-looking causal mechanism. The absence of an instrument, quasi-experiment, or alternative identification strategy means no causal claim is supportable.

**What is the single biggest threat to credibility?**
The Lev_lead falsification failure. In Utility, 5 of 6 Lev_lead models are significant while 0 of 6 Lev_lag models are significant. In Finance, 4 of 6 Lev_lead models significant, 0 of 6 Lev_lag. This pattern is more consistent with reverse causality or confounding than with a leverage-discipline channel.

**What is the single highest-value next fix?**
Report the full 60-model result pattern including all Lev_lead and Lev_t results. This transforms the audit from a document that conceals the falsification failure into one that honestly surfaces it. The thesis can then either reframe the finding or redesign the identification.
