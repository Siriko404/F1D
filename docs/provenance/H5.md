### A3) Outcome / Target Definition

| Variable | Description | Construction | Source |
|----------|-------------|-------------|--------|
| `PostCallDispersion` | Post-call analyst forecast dispersion (%) | SD(next-quarter EPS forecasts) / \|Mean\| × 100, measured +3 trading days after call | IBES Detail, FPI=6, PDF=D |

**Construction details (Druz, Petzev, Wagner & Zeckhauser 2020; Diether, Malloy & Scherbina 2002):**
- **Target horizon:** Next-quarter EPS (FPI='6' — the quarter analysts forecast after current earnings are reported)
- **Dilution:** Diluted EPS only (PDF='D')
- **Post-call timing:** 3 trading days after the earnings call (USFederalHolidayCalendar)
- **Algorithm per call:**
  1. Match call to target FPEDATS (nearest forward within 120-day fence)
  2. Filter IBES Detail estimates to that (gvkey, fpedats)
  3. Keep estimates with actdats ≤ target_date and age ≤ 180 days
  4. Keep latest estimate per analyst
  5. Require ≥ 2 analysts
  6. Compute SD(VALUE) / |Mean(VALUE)| × 100
- **Winsorization:** 1%/99% pooled
- **Pre-call control:** `PreCallDispersion` — same formula, 1 trading day before call

## B. Redesign Rationale

The old H5 was broken — the DV changed 3 times across history (`dispersion_lead` → `dispersion` → `delta_dispersion`), the code expected a column the panel didn't have, and the architecture was outdated (one IV per spec, 3 samples, calendar year FE).

The redesign:
- Constructs the DV properly from the literature (Druz et al. 2020)
- Uses IBES Detail individual analyst estimates (not summary consensus)
- Follows the H1/H4 pattern (6 simultaneous IVs, Industry/Firm FE, Base/Extended controls)
- Main sample only, fiscal year time FE

## C. Red-Team Audit Resolutions

| Finding | Severity | Resolution |
|---------|----------|------------|
| `attach_fyearq()` produces `fyearq` not `fyearq_int` | CRITICAL | Explicit conversion: `np.floor(pd.to_numeric(fyearq)).astype("Int64")` |
| Engine `cols` list missing `PDF` column | CRITICAL | Added `PDF` to `COLUMNS` dict and `cols` list |
| `_find_target_quarter()` no date fence | MAJOR | 120-day fence on FPEDATS matching |
| Singleton cache ignores fpi/pdf params | MAJOR | Added fpi/pdf to cache key |
| `ROABuilder` missing from panel builder | MAJOR | Added to builders list |
| No winsorization of dispersion variables | MAJOR | 1%/99% pooled winsorization |
| Panel needs both `year` and `fyearq_int` | MINOR | Both columns included |
