## Design Decisions

1. **Mirrors H14 pattern:** Base controls = standard 7 + pre_call_amihud (lagged DV, analogous to H14's PreCallSpread).
2. **Extended controls:** Volatility, StockPrice, Turnover (microstructure), Analyst_QA_Uncertainty_pct (linguistic). Parallels H14's extended set.
3. **Dropped from old H7:** Entire_All_Negative_pct (redundant with 6 simultaneous IVs), StockReturn, AmihudIlliq (inter-call level), amihud_change_w5 (robustness variant).
4. **Annual FE:** Switched from call_quarter_int to fyearq_int for consistency with all other suites. H14 (also event-window DV) uses fyearq_int as precedent.
