### A3) Outcome / Target Definition

| Variable | Definition | Timing | Missingness |
|----------|------------|--------|-------------|
| `amihud_illiq` | Contemporaneous Amihud (2002) illiquidity ratio = `mean(|daily_ret| / dollar_volume) * 1e6` computed over call window `[prev_call + 5d, call - 5d]` | t (contemporaneous) | 7,524 calls (6.7%) missing due to insufficient trading days (<10) or no CRSP PERMNO link |

**Amihud Illiquidity Formula:**
```
Amihud_Illiq = mean(|RET_t| / (VOL_t * |PRC_t|)) * 10^6
```
- Computed over trading days in window `[prev_call_date + 5 days, call_date - 5 days]`
- Requires minimum 10 valid trading days
- Zero dollar volume days masked to avoid Inf
