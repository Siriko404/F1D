  <action>
    Score each candidate hypothesis for Red Team prioritization:
    
    1. **Scoring Dimensions (from RESEARCH.md methodology):**
       
       **Novelty (0.35 weight):**
       - 1.0: No prior direct test (TRUE GAP)
       - 0.7: Related tests exist but different operationalization (PARTIAL GAP)
       - 0.4: Incremental extension of existing work
       
       **Feasibility (0.35 weight):**
       - 1.0: All data available, N > 10,000, high within-firm variance
       - 0.8: Minor construction needed, N 5,000-10,000
       - 0.5: Major gaps, N < 5,000, or low within-firm variance
       
       **Confidence (0.30 weight):**
       - 1.0: Strong theoretical prior, effect size plausible
       - 0.8: Moderate prior, reasonable mechanism
       - 0.5: Weak prior, speculative mechanism
    
    2. **Calculate overall score:**
       Score = 0.35 × Novelty + 0.35 × Feasibility + 0.30 × Confidence
       
       Selection threshold: Score >= 0.85 for "extremely high confidence"
    
    3. **Create ranked table:**
       | Rank | ID | Hypothesis | Novelty | Feasibility | Confidence | Score |
       |------|----|-----------:|:-------:|:-----------:|:----------:|:-----:|
       | 1    | H1 | SEC → Call | 1.0     | 0.8         | 0.8        | 0.87  |
       ...
    
    4. **Identify top 10-15 for Red Team:**
       - Anything >= 0.80 passes to Red Team
       - Below 0.80 is "reserve" for later consideration
    
    5. **Document scoring rationale:**
       For each hypothesis in top 15, explain why each dimension scored as it did.
  </action>
