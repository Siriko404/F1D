**run_robustness_checks():**

1. **Alternative FE (Industry + Year):**
   - Replace Firm FE with FF48 Industry FE
   - Same double-clustering (firm, year)
   - Rationale: Tests between-firm effects, matches Biddle (2009) first-stage approach
   - Compare with primary to assess within vs between variation

2. **Alternative DV (|InvestmentResidual|):**
   - Use absolute residual as DV (inefficiency magnitude)
   - Same specification otherwise
   - Rationale: Tests whether compound uncertainty predicts magnitude (not direction) of inefficiency

3. **Lagged IVs (t-1):**
   - Use PRisk_std_t-1, Uncertainty_std_t-1, PRisk_x_Uncertainty_t-1
   - Predict InvestmentResidual_t
   - Rationale: Addresses reverse causality concerns
   - Expect weaker effect if timing matters

4. **Subsample 2006-2018:**
   - Filter to year >= 2006 (exclude sparse early years)
   - Same primary specification
   - Rationale: Early years (2002-2005) have fewer observations, may be noisy

5. **Alternative measures (if time/data permits):**
