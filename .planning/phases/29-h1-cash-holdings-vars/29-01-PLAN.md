# Phase 29 Goal
Construct all dependent, moderator, and control variables for H1 (Cash Holdings) hypothesis.

# Variable Specifications (from REQUIREMENTS.md and CONTEXT.md)

## Dependent Variable (H1-01)
- **Cash Holdings** = CHE / AT
  - CHE: Cash and Cash Equivalents (Compustat annual)
  - AT: Total Assets
  - Scale: Ratio, winsorize at 1%/99%

## Moderator Variable (H1-02)
- **Leverage** = (DLTT + DLC) / AT
  - DLTT: Long-term Debt
  - DLC: Debt in Current Liabilities
  - AT: Total Assets
  - Scale: Ratio, winsorize at 1%/99%

## Control Variables (H1-03, H1-04, H1-05)

### H1-03: Operating Cash Flow Volatility
- **OCF_Volatility** = StdDev(OANCF/AT) over trailing 5 fiscal years
  - OANCF: Operating Cash Flow (annual)
  - AT: Total Assets
  - Window: 5 years, minimum 3 years of data required
  - Computed per firm, rolling forward

### H1-04: Current Ratio
- **Current_Ratio** = ACT / LCT
  - ACT: Current Assets
  - LCT: Current Liabilities

### H1-05: Standard Controls (from existing Step 3 outputs)
- **Tobin's Q** = (AT + Market Equity - CEQ) / AT
  - Market Equity: csho × prcc_f (shares outstanding × price)
  - CEQ: Common/Ordinary Equity
- **ROA** = NI / AT
  - NI: Net Income
- **Capex/AT** = CAPX / AT
  - CAPX: Capital Expenditures
- **Dividend Payer** = Indicator(DVC > 0)
  - DVC: Dividends Common
- **Firm Size** = ln(AT)
  - Natural log of total assets

## Data Treatment (per RESEARCH.md and CONVENTIONS.md)

### Missing Data Handling
- Require complete data for CHE, AT, DLTT, DLC (drop if missing)
- For volatility calculation: require minimum 3 years of data in 5-year window
- Controls from existing Step 3: merge on gvkey/fiscal_year, keep matched only

### Outlier Treatment
- Winsorize all continuous ratios at 1st and 99th percentiles within fiscal year
- This matches standard finance literature practice (CONVENTIONS.md pattern)

### Fiscal Year Alignment
- All variables aligned to fiscal_year (not calendar year)
- Trailing windows use fiscal_year ordering, not calendar date

## Pitfall Prevention (from RESEARCH.md)

1. **FE Collinearity Trap** — Not applicable in variable construction phase (no FE here)
2. **Interaction Multicollinearity** — Not applicable here (centering done in regression phase)

4. **Winsorization Order** — Winsorize after computing ratios, before output
5. **Fiscal vs Calendar Year** — Use fiscal_year consistently for trailing windows
