# Variable Specifications (from REQUIREMENTS.md)

## H1: Cash Holdings
- DV: Cash Holdings = CHE / AT
- Moderator: Firm Leverage = (DLTT + DLC) / AT
- Controls: OCF Volatility (StdDev OANCF/AT, 5yr), Current Ratio (ACT/LCT), Tobin's Q, ROA, Capex/AT, Dividend Payer, Firm Size

## H2: Investment Efficiency
- DV Option 1: Efficiency Score = 1 - (% overinvest + % underinvest years) over 5yr window
  - Overinvest dummy: Capex/Depreciation > 1.5 AND Sales Growth < industry-year median
  - Underinvest dummy: Capex/Depreciation < 0.75 AND Tobin's Q > 1.5
- DV Option 2: Residual from Delta-ROA(t+2) ~ Capex(t)/AT regression (Biddle et al. 2009)
- Controls: Tobin's Q, Cash Flow Volatility, Industry CapEx Intensity, Analyst Dispersion, Firm Size, ROA, FCF, Earnings Volatility

## H3: Payout Policy
- DV 1: Stability = -StdDev(Delta-DPS/mean DPS) over trailing 5 years
- DV 2: Flexibility = % years with |Delta-DPS| > 5% over 5-year window
- Controls: Earnings Volatility, FCF Growth, Firm Maturity, Firm Size, ROA, Tobin's Q, Cash Holdings
