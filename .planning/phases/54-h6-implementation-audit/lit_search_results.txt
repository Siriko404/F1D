## Contradictions to H6 Implementation

### 1. Pre-trends Testing (Freyaldenhoven et al. 2019)
- Issue: Significant future CCCL effects violate parallel trends
- Literature position: Pre-trends violation biases estimator
- H6 position: May be anticipatory effects (Cassell et al. 2021)
- Resolution needed: Document as limitation with anticipatory effects support

### 2. No Contradictions Found For:
- Fixed effects specification (Firm + Year FE is standard)
- Clustering at firm level (Cameron & Miller 2015)
- FDR correction (Benjamini & Hochberg 1995)
- Shift-share construction (follows Borusyak et al. 2024)
- Lagged treatment (standard for causal identification)

## Recommendations for Plans 01-02

- Cite Cassell et al. (2021) for anticipatory effects explanation
